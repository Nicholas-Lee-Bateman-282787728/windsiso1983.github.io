Provide Accounting And Management Audit Services











About:

Hello,


If you are looking for accounting and bookkeeping services, you're in the right place.



I have worked as an auditor with BDO Chartered Accountants - one of the top seven international accounting firms in the world. I have 20 years' experience in consultancy and freelance business.



I specialize in the following areas:



  • Setting up New Company accounts  
  • Managing Chart of Accounts 
  • Accounting Data entry   
  • Accounting journal entries 
  • Bank and Credit card reconciliations 
  • Invoicing 
  • Financial Statements/ reporting - weekly, monthly, quarterly and yearly 
  • Tax - VAT, Sales Tax and Income Tax
  • Business plans and budgeting
  • Recording, categorization and data entry of financial data 
  •  Any other task requested by the client such as converting data files from one Xero to Quickbooks
